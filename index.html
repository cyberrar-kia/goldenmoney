About GoldenMoney

What We Do

GoldenMoney is a Canadian precious metals dealer specialising in the purchase of gold, silver, platinum, and palladium bullion. We serve both first-time buyers and seasoned investors looking for reliable, fairly priced metals.

We carry an extensive range of coins, bars, and rounds from the world's most respected mints and refineries — including the Royal Canadian Mint, PAMP Suisse, and more.

About Gold

Gold has been the world's premier store of value for thousands of years. As a finite resource, it cannot be printed or manufactured, making it a reliable hedge against inflation and currency devaluation.

About Silver

Silver offers investors both monetary value and industrial demand. More affordable than gold per ounce, silver provides accessible entry into precious metals investing with significant upside potential.
